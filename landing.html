Captain's Mode League

Grab a beer and lets play some Dota!

Captains Mode League is a monthly Dota 2 league with a fun atmosphere where we drink beer and have fun. We have an A,B and C division to accommodate players of all skill level. We use a hidden MMR calculator to determine what devission a team will play in. Round robin style matches are played during the month and then in the final week we have knockout matches until we have a winner of each devision who will receive 2 cases of beer and the team will get custom CML 660ml mugs. Grab your team and join now. If you don'y have a team of your own you can contact Ouboet and he will add you to the reqruitement group.
